About us

We are not your average breeder! We focus on creating an experience for the families and puppies that will bring joy for years to come.

How are we different?
  •   Gift Bag: We send home a gift bag that includes the popular Snuggle Puppy and many other goodies to help your pup transition to your home
  •   Communication: Once your adoption application is received, we will provide photos, video, and even Facetime with the puppy as we approach adoption day
  •   Education: We have a ‘Congrats file’ that’s full of great information, including:
  • What is the best way to travel with my puppy?
  • What do I need to know about the drive/flight home?
  • We share tips to make the transition to home as smooth as possible while also creating good long-term habits
  • Grooming guidelines
  • Where do I start with toys?
  • Why crate train?
  •   Home Raised: The puppies are always raised in my home, not in a separate kennel or building.
  •   Socialized: The puppies have many experiences to help them prepare for their new life with you, including other people / pets and activities
  •   Crate Training: Puppies are crate-trained with their Snuggle Puppy and toys before they come to your home to ensure a smooth transition. Here are a few reasons why this is such a huge head start:
  • They will eat their food in their crates and have excellent eating habits
  • Potty training will be well underway
  •   Health Tested: Their parents have been health tested with guaranteed pedigree
  •   Training: Families wishing to do at home training - we are partners with Bella & Baxter which covers everything from the day you bring your puppy home to advanced, you get a discounted rate through us, one fee lifetime access and they are doodle centric. Literally every issue you encounter is covered
  •   Transparency: We encourage our families to connect through our Facebook group. We want people to be able to ask questions and see littermates if they’d like to! We also confess the best part of our days is when we get to see "pupdates" from our families
Our top priorities are to find loving families for our puppies and do as much as we possibly can to set them up for success. We love our dogs and their puppies, and we want them to have the best life they possibly can.
